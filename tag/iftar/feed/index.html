In the last year Mozilla Bangladesh first time arranged MozIftar in Dhaka. It was an interesting event. Iftar basically an Islamic term. In the holy month of Ramadan muslim people fasting the whole day & take meal at the dawn. It is called Iftar. We mozillians from Bangladesh do that thing together with Mozilla mission. And named it MozIftar. From last year we are regularly arranging MozIftar in Bangladesh. This event taken place in Star Kabab (Old Dhaka) on July 29, 2013.

We had some agendas for this meetup:

  • Set a plan & direction for Mozilla Bangladesh for next one year.
  • Build a event calendar.
  • If possible create some program specific task force.
  • Discuss about event plan (cost – benefit & event target).
  • We have some issues to discuss face to face.
  • And ofcourse Iftar :-)

MozIftar Dhaka 2013 Banner

We opened a sign-up form for this event. We found around 70 people signed up for the event. We expected there will be around 50 people in the event. But beyond all expectation we found 70+!! We never thought it can be happen. In the last year MozIftar Dhaka attended by only 10/12 Mozillians. Surely we reached a great momentum since then. Most Importantly this year we had arranged another MozIftar in Chittagong. It was attended by around 40 Mozillians.

MozIftar Dhaka 2013

In the event our senior Mozillian Mahay Alam Khan started the discussion. He emphasized on using mailing list for formal discussion. We discussed about our future goal of Mozilla Bangladesh, the upcoming Mozilla Summit 2013, future event plan & most importantly we decided to propose to set Cox’s Bazar as a venue of South Asian Meet-up in 2014.

MozIftar Dhaka 2013 Group Photo

We also found a lot new enthusiast who’s are interested to contribute in Mozilla. They was pretty curious about the activity of Mozilla Bangladesh. And I found some really interested people from BRAC University. They are interested for a FxOS event in their campus. We had a successful discussion about that.

It was the last event for the MozIftar Bangladesh 2013. We had plan for another MozIftar this year in Cox’s Bazar. But for some unavoidable reason we had to drop Cox’s Bazar as a venue of MozIftar Bangladesh 2013.

This time the occasion is Iftar. It is a holy month of Ramadan. The Muslims people do a day long fasting & break it at the evening. This is the Iftar. In Dhaka there is lots of Iftar party arranged. So why don’t we?

Mozilla Bangladesh arranged a Iftar party for present & future volunteers & contributors of Mozilla. In Dhaka the old town is famous for Iftar. So we arranged the Iftar Party at the old town in a very famous hotel Al – Razzaque. MozIftar was sponsored by Ankur ICT Development Foundation.

In this event 13 people was attended including all the ReMo from Bangladesh. We discussed a list of following items.

  1. Mozilla Bangladesh Website
  2. Future events
  3. Arrange workshop for add-on development, localization, boot2gecko.
  4. We decided to arrange a event on Software Freedom Day
  5. Also have plan to arrange a event in a greater aspect in December 2012 with presence of one key staff from Mozilla. It will be further discussed in the Mozilla Bangladesh mailing list
  6. Getting more volunteers & contributors especially at the non technical end.
  7. ReMo Camp Berlin 2012
  8. Mozilla Bangladesh future plan

Mashkawat Ahsan & Mahay Alam Khan both are ReMo from Bangladesh describe a lot of thing from the discussion list. Mashkawat Ahsan describe his experience about the ReMo Camp Berlin. He discussed the events and discussion sessions arranged there.

After the Iftar we take a walk to ‘Hazir Biriwani’, a well known place for eating ‘Biriwani’. We enjoyed ‘Biriwani’

It was a great event. A great fun with the discussions. We enjoyed a lot and found few more enthusiast.
