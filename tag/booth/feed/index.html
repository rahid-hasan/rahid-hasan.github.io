Cry of a Coder » Booth http://blog.rahidhasan.com Rahid Hasan's Blog Wed, 23 Jul 2014 17:56:29 +0000 en-US hourly 1 http://wordpress.org/?v=3.9.1 Mozilla Booth @ Asian University of Bangladesh http://blog.rahidhasan.com/mozilla-booth-asian-university-of-bangladesh/ http://blog.rahidhasan.com/mozilla-booth-asian-university-of-bangladesh/#comments Fri, 29 Jun 2012 20:42:11 +0000 http://blog.rahidhasan.com/?p=4 It was a beautiful Thursday. For the Mozilla Booth placement me & Ashickur Rahman, ReMo, Mozilla Reps reached in the loby of Asian University of Bangladesh just at 9:30 AM. Ashickur Rahman brought the 19″ LCD borrowed from the our favourite Mahay Alam Khan. It was a way too secured place. No one except a valid ID card holder can’t access this place. So we was unable to inform or invite anyone

The plan was place a beautiful Mozilla Booth that looks awesome! Already I got the Mozilla table cloth, a horizontal & a vertical banner. So we started the decoration with the banner I got.

And we encountered by a beautiful crowd. That we never imagined . Just we finished the students of Asian University of Bangladesh made a great wall of people in our Mozilla Booth.

 

Me & Ashickur Rahman faced a versatile level of interest. All of them know about Mozilla. But only about Mozilla Firefox. But we let them know its just beyond a beautiful browser. It has a vast number of project for the people. We mainly focused on Mozilla WebFWD, Webmaker & Popcorn. And also let them know how they can contribute to Mozilla & its browser. We encouraged a them to contribute in localization & attached with Mozilla Bangladesh Community. We show them the different websites of Mozilla projects & introduce them. The crowed was amazed to know the beauty of Mozilla. We show them simple way to get connected with the Mozilla Community Bangladesh.

 

The most important things I would like to tell that w made a awesome survey there all the day. It was great. Students of the Asian University of Bangladesh was interested to fill up the survey. But before fill up we maintain some rules. All most all of them never knew about Mozilla projects & Mozilla Community. So first of all we introduced them what is Mozilla? How it works? What it believes? What are there projects? What is Mozilla Bangladesh Community?

We used 3 to 10 minutes to a single survey. So it takes time :-/ . But output was great!! They was happy & excited. We found a total 55 person’s data. I’ll post another post only about this survey.

To make our work faster we 3 laptops & one 19″ LCD. One was used only for presenting all amazing Mozilla videos with the LCD. Two more we used to take survey & let them about all the greatest things of Mozilla.

 

And of course we distribute Mozilla awesome swags with them. The was happy to get the stickers, wrist band & Mozilla Firefox button.

 

At the end of the day we was tired but it was a great fun experience. The whole day we did lots of fun with the crowd. The as nothing like working with the people. Nothing like the working as a volunteer. Nothing like working for Mozilla Community.

It was way too awesome……… Mozillians RockZ \m/
