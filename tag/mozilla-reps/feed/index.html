Cry of a Coder » Mozilla Reps http://blog.rahidhasan.com Rahid Hasan's Blog Wed, 23 Jul 2014 17:56:29 +0000 en-US hourly 1 http://wordpress.org/?v=3.9.1 Mozilla Bangladesh Task Force Meetup 2014 http://blog.rahidhasan.com/mozilla-bangladesh-task-force-meetup-2014/ http://blog.rahidhasan.com/mozilla-bangladesh-task-force-meetup-2014/#comments Sun, 23 Feb 2014 18:08:42 +0000 http://blog.rahidhasan.com/?p=56 What was the greatest moment of Mozilla Bangladesh? If you ask this question before 22nd February I must be puzzled! Their was a few great things we passed in last few years. Especially the Mozilla Summit was too awesome. Also the last year was full of some great events. Its quite tough to find the best moment. But now if you ask me the same question I have no doubt to select that. Yesterday on February 22nd we arranged the very first meetup of our newly formed community task forces. This is the best thing ever happened for our awesome community.

Mozilla Bangladesh Task Force Group Photo

So what was that actually? Not too far from today, one year earlier our community was quite small with limited number of contributors. But within these time we have had a boost of 10x!! Which was quite unexpected. The main reason of that boost is FSA program & Chittagong tour in the mid of last year. To make our community more awesome & dedicated in the end of last year we planned to initiate some community task forces. Which was introduced last month. The details of the task force can be found here.

After the task force formed our community lead Mahay Alam Khan take the initiative to meet all task force members for discussion & plan for 2014. He did a tremendous job to make this happen just within few weeks. Kudos to him & also Ashickur Rahman to make it possible.

Mozilla Bangladesh Task Force Meetup

The meetup taken place in the conference room of Hotel 71 Dhaka. All the Task Force leads, peers & members was invited. Also we invited 8 more mozillians to join us. For exam & other reasons 3 task force members & 1 mozilian was unable to join us. All the task force leads & Reps (Mahay Alam Khan, Mashkawat Ahsan, Belayet Hossain, Ashickur Rahman, Shafiul Azam, Aniruddha Adhikay, Ratul Minhaz, Tanha Islam & Rahid Hasan) was attended. Some folks came to Dhaka after traveling the whole night.

Mahay Alam Khan welcome everybody to join this meetup. Everybody introduced them with their unique gesture. Following that Mahay Alam Khan lead the session “Overview 2014 and Goals from Mozilla”. He recently attended Mozilla San Fransisco work week in January. Aniruddha Adhikary & other folks actively discussed & shared their views regarding this. Just after that each task force started their SWOT analysis. The SWOT analysis of Marketing lead by Mashkawat Ahsan was really eye catchy. Maliha Mamtaz assisted to put all inputs from different task force in a single etherpad. Next we started discussion on these SWOT analysis of different task forces. This session was moderated by Rahid Hasan. A member from each task force presented their SWOT analysis and we shared our views & discussed on it. The SWOT of Community Engagement task force was most important. The most discussed item was “Lone wolf in the community”. It was taken most of the time of the whole session. Recently we have faced some discrete & meaningless activities in different areas. Which harmed our community cohesion. These kind of activities was discussed with highest priority.

SWOT Analysis

After SWOT discussion we have joined in the lunch. That was too good. Ashickur Rahman, Belayet Hosssain, Maliha Momtaz & some other folks took the lead & show us how to eat! Following the lunch we did some group photos on the venue roof top.

The second half was started with presentation “Million Mozillians” by Mahay Alam Khan. It was a pretty interactive session. He discussed what is mozillian & why we need millions of mozillians. Following this we started to build the event calendar for 2014. Mahay Alam Khan was the lead of this session. The draft sketch can be found here.

We had a small experience sharing session at last. This session was mainly for those whose are new or low profiled but did some great things from their places. Forhad, Istiaque, Rabby, Fahia & Sumi expressed their views, success stories & experience with us.

Mozilla Bangladesh Reps

Meetup was ended up with yet another photo session with different task forces & all Mozilla Bangladesh Reps.

In the last year Mozilla Bangladesh first time arranged MozIftar in Dhaka. It was an interesting event. Iftar basically an Islamic term. In the holy month of Ramadan muslim people fasting the whole day & take meal at the dawn. It is called Iftar. We mozillians from Bangladesh do that thing together with Mozilla mission. And named it MozIftar. From last year we are regularly arranging MozIftar in Bangladesh. This event taken place in Star Kabab (Old Dhaka) on July 29, 2013.

We had some agendas for this meetup:

  • Set a plan & direction for Mozilla Bangladesh for next one year.
  • Build a event calendar.
  • If possible create some program specific task force.
  • Discuss about event plan (cost – benefit & event target).
  • We have some issues to discuss face to face.
  • And ofcourse Iftar :-)

MozIftar Dhaka 2013 Banner

We opened a sign-up form for this event. We found around 70 people signed up for the event. We expected there will be around 50 people in the event. But beyond all expectation we found 70+!! We never thought it can be happen. In the last year MozIftar Dhaka attended by only 10/12 Mozillians. Surely we reached a great momentum since then. Most Importantly this year we had arranged another MozIftar in Chittagong. It was attended by around 40 Mozillians.

MozIftar Dhaka 2013

In the event our senior Mozillian Mahay Alam Khan started the discussion. He emphasized on using mailing list for formal discussion. We discussed about our future goal of Mozilla Bangladesh, the upcoming Mozilla Summit 2013, future event plan & most importantly we decided to propose to set Cox’s Bazar as a venue of South Asian Meet-up in 2014.

MozIftar Dhaka 2013 Group Photo

We also found a lot new enthusiast who’s are interested to contribute in Mozilla. They was pretty curious about the activity of Mozilla Bangladesh. And I found some really interested people from BRAC University. They are interested for a FxOS event in their campus. We had a successful discussion about that.

It was the last event for the MozIftar Bangladesh 2013. We had plan for another MozIftar this year in Cox’s Bazar. But for some unavoidable reason we had to drop Cox’s Bazar as a venue of MozIftar Bangladesh 2013.

The banner of Firefox Student Ambassadors Meetup

Firefox Student Ambassadors program just launched recently. With less than a month we successfully organized an event. The very first Student Ambassador event. It was a meetup of Firefox Student Ambassadors & Mozillians of Mozilla Bangladesh Community.

Team AUB Firefox Club

This event organized by AUB Firefox Club with the support of Mozilla Bangladesh. The club lead Zobayer Ahmed Khan take the initiative & play a really crucial role to make it happen.

The background of this event is very interesting. On June 2, 2013 in a FB chat Zobayer asked me to meet with the club members of AUB Firefox Club and give them some direction. I asked him why not do something big & more open? I proposed him to organize a Meetup event for all Firefox Student Ambassadors in Dhaka. He take that challenge & make it happen to oraganize the first Firefox Student Ambassadors Meetup in just 4 days! This is really awesome \m/ . Feels like a Mozillians!!

Primarily we estimate a rough plan of the event. As he is not a Rep, I created the event on Reps portal & asked for the budget. Before that I discuss with my mentor Vineel. He gave me a very positive response. And just the day before the event Soumya Deb gave me the green signal on behalf of the Reps Council that the budget is approved.

My estimation was there will be 25 to 30 attendee. Among them 15 to 20 Student Ambassadors, 5 to 10 Mozillians, 5 to 7 WoMoz & atleast 2 Mozilla Reps will join in this event. But there was also a little bit bad weather on event day. That can hamper those figures. And I’m shocked to see the awesomeness of Mozillians participation!!

I found 35 attendee in the event. Among them 15 Student Ambassador, 5 WoMoz, 18 Mozillian & 2 Mozilla Rep attended. Really unexpected from me. I never thought this crowd. Interestingly two WoMoz (Asma & Mukta) came after about 1.5 hours of journey! What a passion!!

We started our event on 4:30 PM at Uttara Amontron Restaurant. Me (Rahid Hasan) started with a little introduction. I introduce the AUB Firefox Club & its lead Zobayer Ahmed to the audience. Whose are the big guy of this arrangement. Later everyone introduce their name and their University/Institute. Most of the mozillians came from Dhaka Polytechnic Institute. Their was also Mozillians from North South University, American International University – Bangladesh, International University of Business Agriculture & Technology. And ofcourse the organizing team from Asian University of Bangladesh.

Firefox OS Developer Preview Keon demonstration

Then Ashickur Rahman (Mozilla Reps) start the interactive session. We asked them some questions. They response & share their ideas. The gist of that session is here:

Why you joined Fx Student Ambassadors?

  • Promoting Mozilla
  • Contribute to Mozilla
  • Hype
  • Let others informed
  • To know more
  • To become Reps
  • Participate on Events
  • To Follow footsteps
  • Organizing Events

What Can we do?

  • Open a club
  • Support for Firefox
  • Firefox OS app development
  • Engage more people
  • Let other know about different Mozilla project
  • Open a official club on institute

What Kind of Support they might need from Mozilla Bangladesh?

  • Talk with institute authority to organize events if needed
  • logistics support like : budget, swag
Question from mozillians
  • Why the age limit is over 18 to apply as Fx Student Ambassadors?

Yammy!! :D

After that me discuss with them about Mozilla, Mozilla Bangladesh Community, the open web & the purpose of Firefox Student Ambassadors program. There was also Keon Demonatration. Later then we arranged some snacks. That was great. All of us enjoyed a lot.

The awesome Mozillians!

It was a really successful event. The awesome Mozillians made me feel like we are growing fast. And give us the strength to make a dream for something big & better for open web.
