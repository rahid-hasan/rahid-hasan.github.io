MozCoffe (And Keon Showdown!) @ BUET

 

It was just another Firefox OS event in Dhaka. And the very first event for Firefox OS developer preview Keon demonstration. This event arranged in Bangladesh University of Engineering & Technology (BUET). It was jointly organized by Mozilla Bangladesh Community & BSADD group. My heartily welcome to those people to make this event possible. Especially thanks to Shafiul Azam, a ReMo based on Dhaka to take the initiative.

 

Presentation by Shafiul Azam

 

The main purpose of this event was demonstrating the Keon devices which just arrived in Mozilla Bangladesh Community. The event was started on 6:00 PM at Seminar Room, Information Access Center, BUET. Before the event start we demonstrated the Keon to the awesome audiences. They was very keen to have this device built with awesomeness.

 

The awesome audience!

 

At starting the event Mashkawat Ahsan, ReMo Mentor given a speech about the Mozilla Bangladesh. Then Shafiul Azam demonstrated about “Introducing Open Web”. Next Aniruddha Adhikary, ReMo given a speech about the architecture of the Firefox OS.

 

Question & Answer

 

There was about 35 audience attended. Some of them was our volunteer & contributors. All are enjoyed the presentations. We had a few a swag saved from our previous events. We distributed those (Lanyard, Mozilla Button, Pen) to the audience.

 

Swag

 

We had a few snacks arranged. After the event the volunteers distributed those snacks to the audience.

 

After the event we discuss about our upcoming events & future plans. The cost of the event was sponsored by three ReMo.  It was a nice event & I indeed enjoyed a lot.
