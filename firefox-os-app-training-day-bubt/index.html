Firefox OS App Training Day – BUBT

Standard

Organizers of App Train BUBT

As part of our initiate for Firefox OS we have arranged Firefox OS App Train Day at Bangladesh University of Business & Technology. It was May 2, 2014. The initiate was started on early March by Himel & Ashickur Rahman to arrange some MozEvents in BUBT. Later I got involved & found its a perfect campus for a AppTrain event. That time their was NO Firefox club in that University. But soon they got interested & formed BUBT Firefox Club by Shahriar, Shipu Ahmed & some other folks. Later they applied for arrange a day long AppTrain session in the campus. It got approved soon. And get started to make it happen.

Introduction to Firefox OS

On the event day Aniruddha Adhikary, Mohi-Us-Sunnat, Rabbi Hossain & me was present as a trainer. Also Ashickur Rahman, Zobayer Ahmed & Ratul was attended the event. I have started the event with ‘Firefox OS Introduction’. I explained why it is important & how its becoming the future.

Firefox OS Kick Start

On next to me Rabbi Hossain started the session ‘App Development Kick Start’. He describe how easily a Hello World app can be developed in Firefox OS. After that Aniruddha Adhikary started his session ‘Web API’. It was quit interesting & total fun. He started with a funny video. It was quite amusing. Later Mohi-Us-Sunnat present his session about ‘App Manifest’. He described the different parts of a manifest file. Also he shows tricks of it.

App Development Hack Session

After that we call for app ideas from participants. All attendees shared their app ideas as a individual or group. We got a total of 21 app ideas from more than 50 participants. Then we go for prayer & lunch break.

Mozilla swags

After the break we started our hack session. From 3 PM to 5 PM all participants tried develop a demo of their app ideas. It was a great experience. Later all groups come in front their demonstrate their app prototype. We found some interesting apps. Some were really awesome. And we distributed Mozilla swags to them.

All Participants

The day long day long app train was ended with a group photo session. People smiled, shouts & shows their awesomeness!
