Firefox Student Ambassadors Meetup

Standard

The banner of Firefox Student Ambassadors Meetup

Firefox Student Ambassadors program just launched recently. With less than a month we successfully organized an event. The very first Student Ambassador event. It was a meetup of Firefox Student Ambassadors & Mozillians of Mozilla Bangladesh Community.

Team AUB Firefox Club

This event organized by AUB Firefox Club with the support of Mozilla Bangladesh. The club lead Zobayer Ahmed Khan take the initiative & play a really crucial role to make it happen.

The background of this event is very interesting. On June 2, 2013 in a FB chat Zobayer asked me to meet with the club members of AUB Firefox Club and give them some direction. I asked him why not do something big & more open? I proposed him to organize a Meetup event for all Firefox Student Ambassadors in Dhaka. He take that challenge & make it happen to oraganize the first Firefox Student Ambassadors Meetup in just 4 days! This is really awesome \m/ . Feels like a Mozillians!!

Primarily we estimate a rough plan of the event. As he is not a Rep, I created the event on Reps portal & asked for the budget. Before that I discuss with my mentor Vineel. He gave me a very positive response. And just the day before the event Soumya Deb gave me the green signal on behalf of the Reps Council that the budget is approved.

My estimation was there will be 25 to 30 attendee. Among them 15 to 20 Student Ambassadors, 5 to 10 Mozillians, 5 to 7 WoMoz & atleast 2 Mozilla Reps will join in this event. But there was also a little bit bad weather on event day. That can hamper those figures. And I’m shocked to see the awesomeness of Mozillians participation!!

I found 35 attendee in the event. Among them 15 Student Ambassador, 5 WoMoz, 18 Mozillian & 2 Mozilla Rep attended. Really unexpected from me. I never thought this crowd. Interestingly two WoMoz (Asma & Mukta) came after about 1.5 hours of journey! What a passion!!

We started our event on 4:30 PM at Uttara Amontron Restaurant. Me (Rahid Hasan) started with a little introduction. I introduce the AUB Firefox Club & its lead Zobayer Ahmed to the audience. Whose are the big guy of this arrangement. Later everyone introduce their name and their University/Institute. Most of the mozillians came from Dhaka Polytechnic Institute. Their was also Mozillians from North South University, American International University – Bangladesh, International University of Business Agriculture & Technology. And ofcourse the organizing team from Asian University of Bangladesh.

Firefox OS Developer Preview Keon demonstration

Then Ashickur Rahman (Mozilla Reps) start the interactive session. We asked them some questions. They response & share their ideas. The gist of that session is here:

Why you joined Fx Student Ambassadors?

  • Promoting Mozilla
  • Contribute to Mozilla
  • Hype
  • Let others informed
  • To know more
  • To become Reps
  • Participate on Events
  • To Follow footsteps
  • Organizing Events

What Can we do?

  • Open a club
  • Support for Firefox
  • Firefox OS app development
  • Engage more people
  • Let other know about different Mozilla project
  • Open a official club on institute

What Kind of Support they might need from Mozilla Bangladesh?

  • Talk with institute authority to organize events if needed
  • logistics support like : budget, swag
Question from mozillians
  • Why the age limit is over 18 to apply as Fx Student Ambassadors?

Yammy!! :D

After that me discuss with them about Mozilla, Mozilla Bangladesh Community, the open web & the purpose of Firefox Student Ambassadors program. There was also Keon Demonatration. Later then we arranged some snacks. That was great. All of us enjoyed a lot.

The awesome Mozillians!

It was a really successful event. The awesome Mozillians made me feel like we are growing fast. And give us the strength to make a dream for something big & better for open web.
